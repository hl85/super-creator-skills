# storybook-watercolor

Soft hand-painted illustration with whimsical charm

## Atmosphere

Delicate watercolor washes bleed gently into textured paper, creating a dreamy, ethereal glow. Visible brushstrokes carry the warmth of a painter's hand, while soft color bleeds and pigment pools lend an organic, handcrafted feel that whispers of storybook adventures.

## Color Palette

- Primary: Soft watercolor washes - muted blues, greens, warm earth
- Background: Watercolor paper texture, white or cream
- Accents: Deeper pigment pools, splatter effects

## Visual Elements

- Visible brushstrokes
- Soft color bleeds and gradients
- White space as design element
- Delicate line work over washes
- Natural, organic shapes
- Dreamy, atmospheric quality

## Typography

- Elegant hand-lettering
- Watercolor-style text
- Flowing, organic letterforms
- Integrated with illustrations

### Avoid

- Using harsh black outlines or thick borders
- Adding photorealistic details or photographs
- Creating high-contrast, neon color palettes
- Including geometric shapes with sharp, perfect angles
- Using rigid, grid-based layouts

## Example Prompt

A whimsical storytelling infographic about forest animals, rendered in storybook-watercolor style: soft watercolor washes of muted blues, greens, and warm earth tones, textured watercolor paper background, visible brushstrokes, delicate line work over color bleeds, natural organic shapes, elegant hand-lettered text, portrait (9:16) aspect ratio

## Best For

Storytelling, emotional journeys, nature topics, children's education, artistic presentations
