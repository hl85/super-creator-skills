# kawaii

Japanese cute style with big eyes and pastel colors

## Atmosphere

A cozy bedroom filled with plush stuffed animals, pastel pink and mint green blankets, and fairy lights twinkling softly. Sunlight filters through sheer curtains, casting a warm, gentle glow. The air feels playful and comforting, like a hug from a favorite childhood toy, with sparkles dancing in the air and cute little hearts everywhere.

## Style Rules

### Do

- Big sparkly eyes on characters
- Rounded, soft shapes
- Blushing cheeks
- Sparkles and stars scattered
- Cute animal characters
- Chibi proportions

### Avoid

- Sharp, angular edges or geometric shapes
- Dark, moody color palettes without pastels
- Photorealistic images or photographs of real people/animals
- Serious or intense facial expressions without smiles or blushes
- Small, realistic eyes without sparkles or extra cuteness
- Minimalist, plain designs without sparkles, stars, or heart decorations

## Color Palette

- Primary: Soft pastels - pink (#FFB6C1), mint (#98D8C8), lavender (#E6E6FA)
- Background: Light pink or cream, sparkle overlays
- Accents: Bright pops, star and heart shapes

## Visual Elements

- Big sparkly eyes on characters
- Rounded, soft shapes
- Blushing cheeks
- Sparkles and stars scattered
- Cute animal characters
- Chibi proportions

## Typography

- Rounded, bubbly fonts
- Cute decorations on letters
- Hearts and stars in text
- Soft, friendly appearance

## Example Prompt

Kawaii style infographic explaining the life cycle of a butterfly, featuring big sparkly eyes on cute chibi butterfly characters, rounded soft shapes, blushing cheeks, sparkles and stars scattered everywhere, soft pastel pink, mint, and lavender color palette, light cream background with sparkle overlays, rounded bubbly fonts with cute decorations—no sharp edges, no dark colors, no photorealistic images.

## Best For

Cute tutorials, children's education, lifestyle content, character-driven explanations
