# ikea-manual

Minimal line art assembly instruction style

## Atmosphere

A crisp white assembly manual lies flat on a light wooden workbench, illuminated by bright, even overhead lighting. The pages are clean and uncluttered, with simple black line drawings and minimal text. The air feels organized and practical, like a calm, focused moment of putting something together step by step.

## Style Rules

### Do

- Simple line drawings
- Numbered step sequences
- Arrow indicators
- Exploded assembly views
- Wordless communication
- Stick figures for scale

### Avoid

- Photorealistic images or photographs of real products
- Complex shading, gradients, or colorful fills
- Excessive text or detailed paragraphs
- Cartoonish or stylized figures that aren't simple stick figures
- Cluttered compositions with too many elements
- Bright, neon colors that distract from the minimal instruction style

## Color Palette

- Primary: Black lines, minimal fills
- Background: White or cream paper
- Accents: Red for warnings, blue for highlights

## Visual Elements

- Simple line drawings
- Numbered step sequences
- Arrow indicators
- Exploded assembly views
- Wordless communication
- Stick figures for scale

## Typography

- Minimal text
- Step numbers prominent
- Universal symbols
- Simple sans-serif when needed

## Example Prompt

IKEA manual style infographic explaining how to brew pour-over coffee, featuring simple black line drawings on white paper background, numbered step sequences, arrow indicators, exploded views of coffee equipment, wordless communication, stick figures for scale, minimal text with prominent step numbers, simple sans-serif font when needed—no photorealistic images, no complex shading, no excessive text.

## Best For

Step-by-step instructions, assembly guides, how-to content, universal communication
