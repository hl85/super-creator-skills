# aged-academia

Historical scientific illustration with aged paper aesthetic.

## Atmosphere

The smell of yellowed parchment and old leather bindings fills the air. Cross-hatched ink lines dance beneath sepia tones, while faint iron gall ink spots whisper of centuries past. The texture feels like a well-worn notebook passed through generations of curious hands.

## Color Palette

- Primary: Sepia brown (#704214), aged ink, muted earth tones
- Background: Parchment (#F4E4BC), yellowed paper texture
- Accents: Faded red annotations, iron gall ink spots

## Variants

| Variant | Focus | Visual Emphasis |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| **Notebook** | Personal sketches, inventions | Cursive notes, margin annotations |
| **Specimen** | Scientific classification | Numbered diagrams, Latin labels |

## Visual Elements

- Aged paper texture overlay
- Detailed cross-hatching and line work
- Scientific illustration precision
- Study notes and annotations
- Specimen plate or sketch aesthetic
- Numbered diagram elements

## Typography

- Handwritten cursive or serif fonts
- Scientific annotations
- Small caps for labels
- Italics for scientific names

## Style Rules

### Do

- Use cross-hatching and detailed line work for shading
- Include handwritten margin notes and cursive annotations
- Add subtle paper texture overlays and ink spot effects
- Maintain scientific illustration precision in diagrams
- Use sepia tones and muted earth colors throughout

### Avoid

- Using bright, saturated digital colors
- Creating perfectly clean, vector-style lines
- Adding photorealistic elements or photographs
- Using modern sans-serif fonts exclusively
- Including glossy effects or gradients

## Example Prompt

A historical scientific infographic explaining the anatomy of a fern, rendered in aged-academia style: detailed cross-hatched ink illustrations on yellowed parchment background, handwritten cursive margin notes in iron gall ink, Latin labels in small caps italics, subtle paper texture with faint coffee stains, sepia color palette, specimen plate aesthetic, 16:9 aspect ratio

## Best For

Scientific education, biology topics, historical explanations, inventions, nature documentation
