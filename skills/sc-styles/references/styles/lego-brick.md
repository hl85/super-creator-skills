# lego-brick

Toy brick construction with playful aesthetic

## Atmosphere

The satisfying click of plastic studs locking together echoes with creative possibility. Bright primary colors pop against light gray baseplates, while minifigure faces hint at stories waiting to be built. The texture feels smooth and sturdy, like a childhood imagination made tangible.

## Color Palette

- Primary: Classic LEGO colors - red, blue, yellow, green, white
- Background: Light gray baseplate or white
- Accents: Bright primary pops, shiny studs

## Visual Elements

- Visible brick studs
- Modular construction
- Minifigure characters
- Building instruction style
- Stackable elements
- Plastic sheen

## Typography

- Blocky, bold fonts
- LEGO instruction style
- Step numbers
- Playful appearance

## Style Rules

### Do

- Use visible brick studs on all elements
- Maintain modular construction aesthetic
- Include minifigure characters where appropriate
- Add subtle plastic sheen and highlights
- Follow building instruction-style layout

### Avoid

- Using non-LEGO color palettes or gradients
- Creating smooth, non-brick textured surfaces
- Adding photorealistic elements or photographs
- Using rounded or organic shapes instead of blocky forms
- Omitting visible studs on brick elements
