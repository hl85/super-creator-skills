# corporate-memphis

Flat vector people with vibrant geometric fills

## Atmosphere

Bright, bold geometric shapes float playfully against clean white backgrounds. Disproportionate vector figures exude friendly confidence, while vibrant solid colors pop with modern energy. Every element feels crisp, professional, and approachable, like a contemporary office come to life.

## Color Palette

- Primary: Bright, saturated - purple, orange, teal, yellow
- Background: White or light pastels
- Accents: Gradient fills, geometric patterns

## Visual Elements

- Flat vector illustration
- Disproportionate human figures
- Abstract body shapes
- Floating geometric elements
- No outlines, solid fills
- Plant and object accents

## Typography

- Clean sans-serif
- Bold headings
- Professional but friendly
- Minimal decoration

## Style Rules

### Do

- Use flat vector illustrations with no outlines
- Create disproportionate human figures with abstract body shapes
- Incorporate floating geometric elements as accents
- Use bright, saturated solid colors (purple, orange, teal, yellow)
- Include simple plant and object accents

### Avoid

- Adding outlines or strokes to any elements
- Using realistic proportions or photorealistic elements
- Creating complex gradients or shading
- Including excessive decorative details or textures
- Using serif fonts or overly decorative typography

## Example Prompt

A business infographic explaining company growth, rendered in corporate-memphis style: flat vector illustrations with no outlines, disproportionate human figures with abstract body shapes, floating geometric elements, bright saturated colors (purple, orange, teal, yellow), white background, simple plant accents, clean sans-serif typography, bold headings, 16:9 aspect ratio

## Best For

Business presentations, tech products, marketing materials, corporate training
