# knolling

Organized flat-lay with top-down arrangement

## Atmosphere

A perfectly organized flat-lay arrangement on a solid white surface, photographed from directly above. Every object is placed at precise 90-degree angles, with equal spacing between each item. The lighting is even and soft, casting subtle shadows that give depth without clutter. The scene feels satisfyingly orderly, like a perfectly curated catalog spread.

## Style Rules

### Do

- Top-down camera angle
- Objects arranged at 90° angles
- Equal spacing between items
- Clean organization
- Symmetry and order
- No overlapping items

### Avoid

- Photographs taken from eye level or angled perspectives
- Objects placed at random, non-90-degree angles
- Cluttered arrangements with overlapping items
- Uneven spacing between objects
- Busy, patterned backgrounds that distract from the objects
- Excessive decorations or elements that break the clean, organized feel

## Color Palette

- Primary: Object's natural colors
- Background: Solid color - black, white, or colored surface
- Accents: Shadows, subtle highlights

## Visual Elements

- Top-down camera angle
- Objects arranged at 90° angles
- Equal spacing between items
- Clean organization
- Symmetry and order
- No overlapping items

## Typography

- Clean labels
- Positioned outside objects
- Connecting lines to items
- Minimal, catalog-style
