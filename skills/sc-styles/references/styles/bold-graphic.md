# bold-graphic

High-contrast comic style with bold outlines and dramatic visuals.

## Atmosphere

Bold black outlines crackle with energy against bright, unapologetic colors. Halftone dots pulse like comic book printing, while action lines slice through the frame. Every element demands attention with the dramatic flair of a vintage superhero comic.

## Color Palette

- Primary: Bold primaries - red, yellow, blue, black
- Background: White, halftone patterns, dramatic shadows
- Accents: Spot colors, neon highlights

## Variants

| Variant | Focus | Visual Emphasis |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| **Graphic-novel** | Dramatic narratives | Action lines, hatching, panels |
| **Pop-art** | High-energy impact | Halftone dots, Warhol repetition |

## Visual Elements

- Bold black outlines
- High contrast compositions
- Halftone dot patterns
- Comic panel borders optional
- Action lines and motion
- Speech bubbles and sound effects

## Typography

- Comic book lettering
- Impact fonts for emphasis
- POW/BANG effects for pop-art
- Caption boxes for narrative

## Style Rules

### Do

- Use thick, bold black outlines around all elements
- Incorporate halftone dot patterns and ben-day dots
- Add dynamic action lines and speed effects
- Include speech bubbles, sound effects, and caption boxes
- Embrace high-contrast color combinations

### Avoid

- Using thin, delicate lines or outlines
- Creating soft, subtle gradients or shadows
- Adding photorealistic elements or photographs
- Using muted, desaturated color palettes
- Including excessive decorative flourishes

## Example Prompt

A high-impact marketing infographic comparing product features, rendered in bold-graphic style: thick black outlines, halftone dot patterns, action lines slicing through the composition, speech bubbles with bold comic book lettering, POW/BANG sound effects, bright primary colors (red, yellow, blue, black), white background with dramatic shadows, 16:9 aspect ratio

## Best For

Attention-grabbing content, dramatic narratives, pop culture, marketing, high-energy presentations
