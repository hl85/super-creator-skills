# origami

Folded paper forms with geometric precision

## Atmosphere

Crisp creases cut through smooth origami paper, catching light at precise angles. Solid colors glow with quiet confidence, while soft shadows reveal the depth of each fold. The texture feels like carefully folded paper, sharp and clean with a hint of fibrous detail.

## Color Palette

- Primary: Solid origami paper colors - red, blue, green, gold
- Background: White or soft gray, subtle shadows
- Accents: Paper fold highlights, crisp shadows

## Visual Elements

- Geometric folded shapes
- Visible fold lines
- Cast shadows showing depth
- Paper texture
- Angular, faceted forms
- Low-poly aesthetic

## Typography

- Clean geometric fonts
- Angular letterforms
- Folded paper text effect
- Minimal, precise labels

## Style Rules

### Do

- Show visible fold lines on all paper forms
- Use geometric, angular, faceted shapes
- Add soft shadows to show depth of folds
- Maintain paper texture and crisp creases
- Use solid origami paper colors without gradients

### Avoid

- Using rounded or organic shapes
- Adding photorealistic elements or photographs
- Omitting visible fold lines
- Using glossy or non-paper textures
- Creating overly complex curved forms

Geometric concepts, transformation topics, Japanese themes, abstract representations
