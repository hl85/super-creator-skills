# cyberpunk-neon

Neon glow on dark backgrounds, futuristic aesthetic

## Atmosphere

Rain drips down wet asphalt streets in a dystopian megacity at night, reflecting neon pink and cyan signs that flicker and buzz. Holographic advertisements float in the air above, casting colorful glows on chrome surfaces. The air hums with electronic energy, and every corner feels like a portal to a high-tech future.

## Style Rules

### Do

- Glowing neon outlines
- Dark atmospheric backgrounds
- Digital glitch effects
- Circuit patterns
- Holographic elements
- Rain and reflections

### Avoid

- Bright, sunlit environments that wash out neon glow
- Warm, earthy color palettes that contradict the cyberpunk mood
- Photographs of real, non-futuristic settings
- Soft, organic shapes without sharp, angular edges
- Traditional, handwritten fonts instead of digital/tech typefaces
- Clean, perfect surfaces without rain, scratches, or digital imperfections

## Color Palette

- Primary: Neon pink (#FF00FF), cyan (#00FFFF), electric blue
- Background: Deep black (#0A0A0A), dark purple gradients
- Accents: Neon glow effects, chrome reflections

## Visual Elements

- Glowing neon outlines
- Dark atmospheric backgrounds
- Digital glitch effects
- Circuit patterns
- Holographic elements
- Rain and reflections

## Typography

- Glowing neon text
- Digital/tech fonts
- Flickering effects
- Outlined glow letters

## Example Prompt

Cyberpunk-neon style infographic explaining neural interface technology, featuring glowing neon pink and cyan outlines, deep black background with dark purple gradients, digital glitch effects, circuit patterns, holographic elements, rain on wet asphalt reflections, digital tech fonts with flickering effects—no sunlit environments, no warm earthy colors, no traditional handwritten fonts.

## Best For

Tech futures, gaming content, digital culture, futuristic concepts, night aesthetics
