# pastel

Gentle, whimsical, soft

## Atmosphere

The soft warmth of a sunlit nursery, gentle pastel hues on plush fabrics, and the delicate sparkle of fairy lights. It feels gentle, whimsical, and comforting, with a sense of playfulness and innocence that brings a smile to the face.
